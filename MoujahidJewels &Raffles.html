EXCLUSIVE
PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Between:

Raffles Hotel Doha, represented by the Executive Management Board

and

MoujahidJewels, represented by Mr. Moujahid Adam, Deputy Executive Director

Effective Date: Monday, November 3, 2025

Duration: Two (2) years, renewable upon mutual agreement

This new agreement hereby supersedes and fully replaces the previous contract signed between Raffles Hotel Doha and MoujahidJewels, which is declared null and void as of the effective date stated above.

All terms, rights, and obligations shall henceforth be governed exclusively by the provisions contained in the present agreement.

1. Purpose of the Agreement

This agreement establishes a renewed exclusive partnership between MoujahidJewels and Raffles Hotel Doha for the continuation of a luxury jewelry boutique within the hotel, combining the heritage of fine craftsmanship with the prestige of world-class hospitality.

2. Exclusivity of Presence

MoujahidJewels shall maintain exclusive rights to operate the only fine jewelry boutique inside Raffles Hotel Doha and its affiliated establishments within Qatar.

No competing jewelry or watch brand shall be permitted to sell, exhibit, or promote within the property during the agreement term.

3. Financial Terms

  • Raffles Hotel Doha guarantees a minimum monthly profit of thirteen million euros (€13,000,000) to MoujahidJewels.
  • The guaranteed amount may be increased biannually based on boutique performance, guest engagement, and collaborative event outcomes.
  • Marketing, maintenance, and operational logistics within the hotel are fully covered by Raffles Hotel Doha.
  • MoujahidJewels contributes only a symbolic operational fee.

4. Royal Access and Privileges

  • MoujahidJewelsʼ executives hold permanent privileged access to the Royal Suite at Raffles Hotel Doha.
  • These privileges extend to private use for high-level clientele presentations and exclusive jewelry unveilings.
  • The same benefits apply within the Royal Suites of other Raffles establishments worldwide, subject to availability.

5. Boutique Identity and Artistic Control

The boutiqueʼs interior design, lighting concept, and display layout remain entirely under the creative authority of MoujahidJewels.

All decorative elements and visual materials shall reflect the Maisonʼs distinctive artistic signature while aligning with Rafflesʼ luxury standards.

6. The Raffles Signature Collection

MoujahidJewels shall produce an exclusive high jewelry line entitled ʼThe Raffles Signature Collection by MoujahidJewels.ʼ

These pieces will be available solely at Raffles Hotel Doha and handcrafted in limited quantities, emphasizing craftsmanship, rarity, and timeless value.

7. Events and Private Exhibitions

  • Four annual private exhibitions will be organized within Raffles Hotel Doha, curated jointly by both entities.
  • Each event will combine haute joaillerie presentations, fine dining experiences, and VIP client engagement.
  • Guests will be invited from the hotelʼs top-tier clientele and MoujahidJewelsʼ international private list.

8. Training and Staff Excellence

All boutique personnel will undergo continuous training by MoujahidJewelsʼ experts, ensuring product mastery and impeccable customer service.

Dress code, communication standards, and presentation will be defined by MoujahidJewels to align with its brand philosophy.

9. Personalized Client Services

A Private Concierge Program will allow select Raffles guests to request bespoke jewelry design, repair, and customization directly through MoujahidJewelsʼ atelier.

Appointments may be conducted in the boutique or within the Royal Suite for privacy and discretion.

10. Performance Review

Every six (6) months, a joint committee composed of Raffles management and MoujahidJewels representatives will review boutique performance, client satisfaction, and collaboration impact.

Positive performance will trigger the option to extend or enhance the partnership automatically.

11. Exclusive Aviation Partnership with LuxuriaSky

This collaboration unites Raffles Hotel Doha, MoujahidJewels and LuxuriaSky Aviation a subsidiary of the Moujahid Group to create a fully integrated travel and hospitality alliance dedicated to elite global clientele.

Under this agreement:

  • Raffles Hotel Doha shall grant exclusive aviation priority to LuxuriaSky Aviation for all private flight services related to its guests and VIP visitors. No other private aviation company shall be promoted or recommended by the hotel throughout the duration of this partnership.
  • Guests booking stays or experiences at Raffles through LuxuriaSky Aviation will enjoy personalized benefits including priority suite upgrades, direct airport transfers, and private welcoming services coordinated with MoujahidJewels.
  • LuxuriaSky Aviation guarantees the permanent availability of aircraft for Raffles guests, ensuring uninterrupted flight operations with no service shortage.
  • Raffles Hotel Doha shall allocate a 37% commission to LuxuriaSky Aviation for each reservation or booking originating through its aviation clientele.
  • In reciprocity, LuxuriaSky Aviation agrees to recommend and transport its VIP passengers exclusively to Raffles Hotels worldwide, except in destinations where no Raffles establishment exists.

This partnership strengthens the seamless connection between private aviation, luxury hospitality, and fine jewelry, establishing a unified standard of excellence across all Moujahid Group divisions and Raffles properties globally.

12. Shared Profit on Special Events

Profits derived from co-hosted gala dinners, auctions, and exclusive product launches shall be divided between Raffles Hotel Doha (35%) and MoujahidJewels (65%).

This ensures fair recognition of MoujahidJewelsʼ creative and operational leadership.

13. Confidential Client Registry

Both parties agree to maintain a confidential shared client list limited to VIP guests attending MoujahidJewels events.

Data shall be used exclusively for boutique operations and high-level event invitations.

14. Expansion Opportunities

Raffles Hotel Doha shall introduce MoujahidJewels as a preferred jewelry partner to other Raffles properties internationally, facilitating boutique installations and brand collaborations in select future hotels.

15. Ethical and Sustainable Commitment

Both entities commit to ethical sourcing, responsible craftsmanship, and environmentally conscious business practices.

Annual sustainability reports will be exchanged between parties to ensure compliance with luxury industry standards.

16. Renewal Conditions

If boutique performance exceeds agreed expectations for two consecutive quarters, MoujahidJewels will automatically secure a two-year renewal option under improved financial terms.

17. Termination Clause

Either party may terminate this contract with a 60-day written notice in case of material breach.

Unjustified termination will result in a compensatory fee equivalent to six months of guaranteed profit owed to MoujahidJewels.
